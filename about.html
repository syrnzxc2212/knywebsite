About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of student developers who created this website to promote leadership development and community involvement in Caloocan City. This platform aims to provide accessible information, encourage civic participation, and empower individual especially the youth to engage in volunteerism, local programs, and community initiatives.

Mission

KAYA NATIN YOUTH CALOOCAN is youth-empowered, non-profit, and non-governmental organization in Caloocan that champions good governance, social justice, and inclusive development. KYNC aims to encourage the youth in Caloocan for a more proactive citizen participation and nation-building. This organization’s mission is to continuously provide programs, projects, and activities for the well-being of the youth.

Vision

To mobilize young people of Caloocan in community-building, advocacy, and policy engagement that shapes a better future for all. KYNC also envisions a more progressive and participatory youth leadership towards good governance, transparency, accountability, and ethical leadership.
